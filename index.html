about jcl clinics:

JCL Guitar Clinics are an informal musical meet up between you the musicians, and us the instructors. We host a medium-sized audience mostly bi-monthly, and answer all types of questions relating to each of our styles and techniques. We hope by coming along that you will improve your own skill sets and your understanding of music and composition. We perform entire pieces from our own repertoires, and demonstrate techniques for our audiences to observe. The objective is for you to learn from us and to have fun and to meet up with fellow musicians!


We have quite a line up of instructors here at JCL. Such a line up in fact, that it almost seems unbelievable! And in case you're wondering - JCL stands for 'Just Come Learn', and it's COMPLETELY FREE to attend. Unbelievable! We try to host our clinics at least once in every 2 month period but we do not have set dates to give in advance. We also like to give everybody a chance to attend one of our clinics and like to have a first come first served policy.


If you think that all of this sounds good to you, then check our registration page regularly! The registration will be open 3 days in advance of the clinic so don't forget to keep checking as places are limited! When you have registered we will contact you with all of the info for the upcoming clinic.



We look forward to meeting you!
